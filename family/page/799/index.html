    revolUXion + schedule

  • February 4th, 2007

    the revolUXion has begun!

    The first show is up!  We start by trying to level set the state of the art, and then jump in and make something right away.  Dax shows me how easy it is to use Blend to DataBind an RSS feed from my flickr page into a WPF application.  Painfully easy!

    The premise of the show is that Dax (a designer) is going to teach me (a developer) about WPF, WPF/E, and XAML.  We'll also talk about way's for a "developer" and a "designer" to work together.

    We are rank beginners with this, so any feedback or email (revoluxions [at] nukeation [dot] com) would be greatly appreciated!

     

    Join us in the the revoluxions!
