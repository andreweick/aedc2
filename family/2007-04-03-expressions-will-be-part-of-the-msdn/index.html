Expressions will be part of the MSDN

As Dax and I have been saying over on revolUXions, MS has listened to the feedback and will be releasing Expressions as part of the MSDN network.  I'm not surprised at all – in the last few years MS has really turned into a company that listens to the community well (Team System pricing, etc).

Kudo's, though, for listening!
