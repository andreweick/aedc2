January in Washington

Jasmine laughing like crazy while Libby tries to pose.Jasmine poses against a dark background.Libby lays down and looks into the light.It's been snow and snow and snow in D.C.  Last year I think it snowed once, and the snow didn't stay past noon, this year the girls have been out of school for over a week straight.  Obama shut down the government for a week (and he works at home, so it must have been bad).  The subway was closed, our subdivision wasn't plowed for a week, and we lost power for 2 full days.
