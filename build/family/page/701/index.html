    My so called digital life

    This my just be the developer in me coming out, but just like to have everything neat and tiddy (all my classes deconstructed, all my transactions committed).  So I tried to make one RSS feed that contains all my digital leavings.  Currently I have my family blog (RSS), my professional blog (RSS), and my tumble blog (RSS) (not to mention my flickr, and youtube).  I used yahoo pipes to combine all the data into one feed.  Pipes has a fun interface, but I think they missed the mark in terms of ease of use – using terms like "union", "for each annotation", and "regex" they've kind of limited the user base to programmers, and if your a programmer, you write a simple script to combine the feeds anyway….

     

    It is a fun interface though!
